The 3 Best Movies of 2023

Spider-Man: Across the Spider-Verse

Imdb : 8.6

Director : Joaquim Dos Santos, Kemp Powers, Justin K. Thompson

Writers : Phil Lord, Christopher Miller, Dave Callaham

Spider-Man: Into the Spider-Verse is about the adventures of Miles Morales, who steps into a completely different world and is equipped with special abilities after being bitten by a radioactive spider. Reunited with Gwen Stacy, Miles crosses the Multiverses. Meanwhile, Miles encounters the Spider-People, who are responsible for protecting this place no matter what. When the heroes face a big threat and don't know what to do, Miles finds himself against the other Spiders. Miles must use his abilities to protect the people he loves most from a grave threat.



Oppenheimer

Imdb : 8.4

Director : Christopher Nolan

Writers : Christopher Nolan, Kai Bird, Martin Sherwin

Focusing on the life of American physicist Julius Robert Oppenheimer, the film reveals his role in the development of the atomic bomb during World War II. When physicist Julius Robert Oppenheimer assumed scientific leadership of the Manhattan Project during World War II, he and his wife, Kitty, could not have imagined that Oppenheimer's work would have such a profound impact not only on their own lives but on the entire world. . Oppenheimer is called the "father of the atomic bomb" after he and his team worked for a long time to develop nuclear weapons at the Los Alamos National Laboratory in New Mexico. However, the use of his fake invention in Hiroshima and Nagasaki caused Oppenheimer to walk away from the project. As the war drew to a close, Robert Oppenheimer, whose father Lewis Strauss may have founded the US Atomic Energy Agency, opposed international control of nuclear energy and the nuclear arms race and was therefore targeted by the US.

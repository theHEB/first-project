Fast and Furious 9

Director: Justin Lin
Scriptwriter: Daniel Casey, Justin Lin

Summary

Fast and Furious 9 tells the story of Dom and his team, who have to fight against a threat from their past. Dominic Toretto's only priority now is to protect his son Brian. Toretto, who lives a quiet life away from the public eye with his son and Letty, cannot get rid of his past even if he wants to. Forced to confront his past this time, Toretto is forced to fight against his abandoned brother Jakob, who works with the cybercriminal Cipher. Reunited with his team, Dom embarks on a fierce battle to stop the world-destroying plan spearheaded by his brother.
